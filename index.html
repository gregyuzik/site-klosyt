Features

✨

AI Stylist

Weather-aware outfit suggestions and dress advice — “Light jacket,” “Bring an umbrella” — built from your Klosyt. On-device AI uses color theory and seasonal awareness to help you look your best in any weather.

🌌

Sky Vibes

Procedural moon phases, time-of-day skies, and a particle overlay that drifts across the whole app — blossoms, autumn leaves, fireflies, aurora — plus 22 holiday effects from Halloween pumpkins to Valentine's hearts that surface on the day.

👔

Outfits

Drag and drop items to create looks. Save favorites and share beautiful Pantone-style cards. Build a personal lookbook of capsule wardrobe combinations you can revisit anytime.

📅

Plan & Track

Schedule planned outfits to your Apple Calendar, or log what you wore each day. Patterns emerge so you never repeat an outfit by accident, and every item in your closet pulls its weight.

🔐

Private by Design

Everything runs on-device. Your data syncs only to your iCloud — never shared with anyone. No app analytics, no app tracking, no ads — your virtual wardrobe stays completely private.

☁️

Synced Everywhere

iCloud keeps your Klosyt in sync across every Apple device, instantly and automatically. One wardrobe across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Vision Pro.

🎨

Themes & Wallpapers

Curated themes and wallpapers tuned for Dark Mode. Free for everyone — no subscription required — and synced across every Apple device through iCloud.

🔍

Smart Filters

Find any item by season, color, category, material, or pattern in seconds. Quickly narrow down your digital closet to find exactly what you need.

📤

Share Cards

Pantone-style Post and Story cards tagged with real-time weather, location, pattern, and material — plus a scannable App Store QR. Every share looks fashion-editorial.

⚡

Smart Share

The For-Today strip surfaces items matched to today’s weather, so your shareable cards always fit the forecast. Included with Klosyt+.

📱

Widgets

Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets show today’s weather-matched outfit without opening the app. They refresh automatically as the forecast changes. Included with Klosyt+.

🧠

Siri & Apple Intelligence

Ask Siri “What should I wear today?” and get a full outfit pulled from your closet — delivered as a Siri card, not a web search. Powered by on-device Apple Intelligence so nothing leaves your iPhone.

📥

Add Anywhere

Snap a photo, import from your camera roll, paste a store URL, or share straight from Safari, Instagram, or any app. Backgrounds drop automatically, and the AI classifies category, colors, material, and pattern on the spot.

📦

3D Capture

iPhone Pro with LiDAR scans your garment in the round, producing a true 3D model alongside the standard photo. Walk around the piece and Klosyt stitches the angles into a wardrobe-ready asset.
