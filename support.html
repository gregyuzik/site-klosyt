Frequently Asked Questions

📸

Taking the perfect pictures

For the best results when importing clothing, hang your item against a solid, contrasting background. Ensure the lighting is bright and even to help our AI accurately remove the background and capture the true color.

📥

How to Import

You can add items by taking a new photo directly in the app, importing existing photos from your camera roll, or pasting URLs from your favorite clothing stores. You can also share directly from any app — for example, browse a store in Safari and tap Share to send the URL or image straight to Klosyt. As long as it's a URL or image, Klosyt can import it. Klosyt automatically drops the background so your digital closet looks pristine.

👗

Making Outfits

Head to the Outfits tab to mix and match your tops, bottoms, and shoes. You can drag and organize layers exactly how you wear them, and save your favorite combinations for later.

✨

Using AI Suggestions

Don't know what to wear? Tap the AI Stylist button! Our on-device intelligence analyzes realtime weather and your exact wardrobe to suggest the perfect outfit for any activity.

☁️

Backup and Restore

Your Klosyt data is precious. Enable iCloud sync in the app settings to securely back up your entire wardrobe. If you get a new device, simply sign in with the same Apple ID and your closet will seamlessly restore.

📱💻🖥️📺🥽

Multi-Platform

Your digital closet goes wherever you do. Klosyt is available as a native app across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Vision Pro, ensuring you always have your wardrobe at your fingertips.

🏷️

Managing Your Items

Tap any item in your library to view its details. From there you can edit the category, season, color, material, and pattern. Need a new photo? Retake it directly from the detail view. Deleted items go to the trash first, so you can recover them if needed.

📅

Calendar & Planning

Plan your outfits for upcoming days or events using the Calendar tab. You can assign saved outfits to specific dates, track what you wore in the past, and optionally sync with your Apple Calendar so outfit reminders show up alongside your schedule.

🔗

Sharing & Exporting

Share your favorite outfits as beautiful cards with friends. You can also share individual clothing items. Use the iOS Share Extension to import images or URLs directly from Safari, Instagram, or any other app — just tap Share and choose Klosyt.

🔍

Filtering & Sorting

Klosyt's GelCap filter system lets you narrow your wardrobe instantly. Filter by category, season, color, material, or pattern with a single tap. Use the search bar to find any item by name. Switch between grid and wardrobe views depending on how you like to browse.

🎨

Themes & Wallpapers

Make Klosyt your own! With Klosyt+, you can choose from a variety of stunning wallpapers and themes to customize the look & feel of your app. Your chosen theme syncs across all your Apple devices.

📦

Object Capture (3D Scanning)

On iPhone Pro models with LiDAR, you can create stunning 3D scans of your clothing items. Walk around the item to capture it from every angle. The 3D model is saved alongside the standard photo in your library.

Getting Started

1

Add your first item

Tap the + button on the Klosyt tab. Take a photo, choose one from your camera roll, or paste a URL from a clothing store. Klosyt automatically removes the background so your library looks clean and polished.

2

Organize automatically

After importing, Klosyt's AI detects the item's category, color, pattern, and more. You can adjust any details manually if you'd like. Add a season tag so the AI knows when to suggest it.

3

Build your first outfit

Head to the Outfits tab and tap +. Pick a top, bottom, shoes, and any accessories from your library. Drag to reorder layers, then save your look. You can save as many outfits as you want.

4

Get AI suggestions

Open the AI Stylist tab, choose an activity, and let Klosyt analyze the current weather and your wardrobe to suggest the perfect outfit. The more items you add, the better the suggestions become!

Tips & Tricks

💡

Better background removal

Hang items on a contrasting background — a white shirt on a dark door, or a dark jacket against a light wall. Avoid busy patterns behind the item.

📤

Import from any shopping app

Browsing online? Tap the Share button in Safari, Instagram, or any app and choose Klosyt to instantly import the image. No screenshots needed.

📅

Never repeat an outfit

Use the Calendar to track what you've worn. Klosyt remembers, so you can always mix things up and avoid repeating outfits back-to-back.

🧠

More items = smarter AI

The AI Stylist works best with a full wardrobe. Import all your everyday clothing and watch the suggestions get increasingly relevant and creative.

🏷️

Tag seasons accurately

Mark items with the correct season — summer, winter, or all-season. This helps the AI Stylist choose weather-appropriate outfits every time.

☁️

Enable iCloud sync early

Turn on iCloud sync in Settings right away. Your wardrobe will be backed up automatically and available on all your Apple devices.

Troubleshooting

iCloud sync isn't working
Make sure iCloud is enabled for Klosyt in your device's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices. If sync seems stuck, try toggling iCloud off and on for Klosyt. A stable Wi-Fi connection is recommended for the initial sync of large wardrobes.
Background removal isn't ideal
For best results, photograph items against a solid, contrasting background with even lighting. Avoid placing items on busy or patterned surfaces. If the result isn't perfect, you can retake the photo from the item's detail view. Items with colors very similar to the background are the hardest to process.
Weather or location isn't updating
Klosyt uses your location for weather-aware outfit suggestions via Apple WeatherKit. Make sure Location Services are enabled for Klosyt in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. Select "While Using the App" or "Always" to allow weather updates.
How do I restore purchases?
Open Klosyt and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" to reactivate your Klosyt+ subscription. This is helpful after setting up a new device or reinstalling the app. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Items appear blurry or low quality
Klosyt stores full-resolution images for all your items. If images appear blurry, they may still be downloading from iCloud. Ensure a stable internet connection and wait for the sync to complete. Photos taken directly in the app are stored at high resolution.
How do I delete an item?
Tap on any item to open its detail view, then tap the delete button. Deleted items are moved to the Trash, where they can be recovered for 30 days. After that, they are permanently removed. You can access the Trash from Settings.
