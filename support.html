Frequently Asked Questions

📸

Taking the perfect pictures

For the best results when importing clothing, hang your item against a solid, contrasting background. Ensure the lighting is bright and even to help our AI accurately remove the background and capture the true color.

📥

How to Import

Add items by taking a photo, importing from your camera roll, or pasting URLs from clothing stores. You can also share directly from any app — browse in Safari and tap Share to send straight to Klosyt. Klosyt automatically drops the background so your closet looks pristine.

👗

Making Outfits

Head to the Outfits tab to mix and match your tops, bottoms, and shoes. Drag and organize layers exactly how you wear them, and save your favorite combinations for later.

✨

Using AI Suggestions

Don't know what to wear? Tap the AI Stylist button! Our on-device intelligence analyzes realtime weather and your exact wardrobe to suggest the perfect outfit for any activity.

☁️

Backup and Restore

Your Klosyt data is precious. Enable iCloud sync in the app settings to securely back up your entire wardrobe. If you get a new device, sign in with the same Apple ID and your closet will seamlessly restore.

📱

Multi-Platform

Your digital closet goes wherever you do. Klosyt is a native app across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Vision Pro — your wardrobe is always at your fingertips.

🏷️

Managing Your Items

Tap any item to view its details. Edit the category, season, color, material, and pattern. Retake photos directly from the detail view. Deleted items go to the trash first so you can recover them.

📅

Calendar & Planning

Plan your outfits for upcoming days or events using the Calendar tab. Assign saved outfits to specific dates, track what you wore, and sync with your Apple Calendar.

🔗

Sharing & Exporting

Share your favorite outfits as beautiful Pantone-style cards. Use the iOS Share Extension to import images or URLs directly from Safari, Instagram, or any other app.

🔍

Filtering & Sorting

Klosyt's GelCap filter system lets you narrow your wardrobe instantly. Filter by category, season, color, material, or pattern. Search by name and switch between grid and wardrobe views.

🎨

Themes & Wallpapers

Make Klosyt your own! With Klosyt+, choose from stunning wallpapers and themes to customize your app's look & feel. Your chosen theme syncs across all your Apple devices.

📦

Object Capture (3D Scanning)

On iPhone Pro models with LiDAR, create stunning 3D scans of your clothing items. Walk around the item to capture every angle. The 3D model is saved alongside the standard photo.

Troubleshooting

iCloud sync isn't working
Make sure iCloud is enabled for Klosyt in Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices. Try toggling iCloud off and on. A stable Wi-Fi connection is recommended for the initial sync.
Background removal isn't ideal
Photograph items against a solid, contrasting background with even lighting. Avoid busy or patterned surfaces. You can retake the photo from the item's detail view.
Weather or location isn't updating
Make sure Location Services are enabled for Klosyt in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. Select "While Using the App" or "Always" to allow weather updates.
How do I restore purchases?
Open Klosyt → Settings → "Restore Purchases" to reactivate your Klosyt+ subscription. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID.
Items appear blurry or low quality
Images may still be downloading from iCloud. Ensure a stable internet connection and wait for the sync to complete. Photos taken in-app are stored at full resolution.
How do I delete an item?
Tap an item → detail view → delete button. Deleted items go to the Trash for 30 days and can be recovered. Access the Trash from Settings.
