Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Our Privacy Promise

Klosyt is owned, operated, and maintained by Gregory Yuzik ("us", or "we").

Your privacy isn't just a feature of Klosyt — it's the foundation. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data. Period.

Klosyt was built from the ground up so that your wardrobe, your photos, and your personal information never leave your device or your personal iCloud account. We cannot see your data, and we designed it that way on purpose.

Zero Data Collection

We want to be completely transparent: Klosyt collects nothing.

No Tracking. No Analytics. No Exceptions.

Klosyt contains zero analytics frameworks, zero advertising SDKs, and zero tracking technologies. We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not build profiles. We do not sell data — because we don't have any to sell.

The Only Exception: Voluntary Diagnostics

If you experience an issue and choose to contact us for support, you may use the in-app diagnostic sharing feature to send us a report. This only happens when you explicitly choose to share it.

Your Data Stays Yours

All of your content is stored exclusively on your device and in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. Your iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and protected by Apple's encryption.

Device Permissions

Klosyt may request the following device permissions. No data from these permissions is ever sent to us:

  • Camera (iOS) — Used to photograph your clothing items.
  • Location — Used for weather-based outfit suggestions through Apple's WeatherKit.
  • Calendar (iOS) — Used to save planned outfits to a dedicated Klosyt calendar.
  • Notifications — Local notifications only (e.g., outfit reminders).

Apple Platform Services

  • Apple WeatherKit — Provides weather data. Processed by Apple's servers, not ours.
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles subscriptions. We never receive your payment details.
  • Apple CloudKit — Syncs your wardrobe through your personal iCloud account.

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions

Klosyt+ is an optional auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights (International)

Regardless of where you live, Klosyt's privacy-first architecture means your rights are inherently protected:

  • Right of Access — All your data is on your device and in your personal iCloud account.
  • Right to Rectification — You can edit or update any data directly within the app.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete items within the app or delete the app itself.
  • Right to Data Portability — Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under your control.
  • Right to Object — Since we perform no processing of your data, there is nothing to object to.

For EEA users, these rights are guaranteed under the GDPR. For Brazilian users, under the LGPD.

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@Klosyt.com.
